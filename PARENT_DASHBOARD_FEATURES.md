# Parent Dashboard - Comprehensive Child Information Management

## Overview

The Parent Dashboard has been completely redesigned to provide comprehensive child information management for therapists and parents. This system allows parents to input detailed information about their children that therapists can use to provide better, more personalized care.

## Key Features

### 🏠 **Tabbed Interface**
The dashboard now features a clean, organized tabbed interface with four main sections:
- **Child Info**: Manage comprehensive child information
- **Progress**: Track child's emotional and therapy progress
- **Chat**: Communicate directly with therapists
- **Settings**: Manage account preferences and notifications

### 👶 **Child Information Management**

#### **Comprehensive Information Categories**

**1. Basic Information**
- Full Name
- Age
- Gender
- Height (cm)
- Weight (kg)
- Blood Group

**2. Medical Information**
- Medical Conditions
- Current Medications
- Allergies

**3. Emotional & Behavioral Information**
- Trigger Points (What causes stress/anxiety)
- Behavioral Patterns
- Communication Style & Preferences

**4. Preferences & Interests**
- Likes & Interests
- Dislikes & Avoidances
- Learning Style & Preferences
- Social Preferences & Comfort Level

**5. Family & Background**
- Family History (Mental health, relevant background)
- School Information & Performance

**6. Therapy & Goals**
- Therapy Goals & Objectives
- Emergency Contacts

**7. Additional Information**
- Additional Notes for Therapist

#### **Smart Form Design**
- **Organized Sections**: Information is grouped into logical categories
- **Multi-line Support**: Complex information fields support multiple lines
- **Visual Indicators**: Each section has clear visual separation
- **Auto-save**: Information is automatically saved to Firebase
- **Validation**: Form validation ensures data quality

## Getting Started

### **For Parents**
1. **Link Child Account**: Use the "Link Child" button to connect your child's account
2. **Complete Information**: Fill out the comprehensive child information form
3. **Regular Updates**: Keep information current as your child grows and changes
4. **Communicate**: Use the chat feature to communicate with therapists

### **For Therapists**
1. **Access Information**: View comprehensive child profiles in your dashboard
2. **Use Information**: Reference child information during therapy sessions
3. **Update Progress**: Add therapy notes and progress updates
4. **Communicate**: Use the chat feature to communicate with parents
